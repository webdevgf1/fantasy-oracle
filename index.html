Why FantasyOracle?

Fantasy managers and bettors react to news too late — injuries, rotations, and insider whispers move markets before headlines drop. FantasyOracle detects live transfer spikes, parses social signals, and correlates them with market data to surface early, credible insights. Get ahead of every lineup leak, avoid red flags early, and spot undervalued Polymarket lines before they correct.

How It Works

We’ve scraped the Fantasy Premier League squads for all 550 Premier League players and identified ~290 fantasy teams owned by players. We also track fantasy teams from club staff across each corresponding club. We monitor their transfers and live-update subs or moves in real time. Our algorithm then cross-references those insider-like signals with Polymarket to surface undervalued or overvalued markets that correlate to the transfer activity.

Example: if Cole Palmer is transferred out by 7 insiders at Chelsea, he’s likely injured or benched. We alert you immediately and flag that Chelsea’s odds may drift. You can act fast in your fantasy league and evaluate related markets before the news is widely priced in.
